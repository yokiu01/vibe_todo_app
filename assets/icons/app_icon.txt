
Second Brain App Icon
====================

Design Elements:
- Shield shape background (dark green #2D5A27)
- Calendar at bottom (white with dark green border)
- Clock at top center (white with dark green border)
- Circular arrow around clock (orange #FF8C00)
- Check mark in calendar
- Play symbol in clock

Colors:
- Dark Green: #2D5A27
- Orange: #FF8C00
- White: #FFFFFF

This icon represents:
- Planning (calendar)
- Time management (clock)
- Productivity cycle (circular arrow)
- Completion (check mark)
- Action/Progress (play symbol)
- Protection/Reliability (shield)
